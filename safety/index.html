Safety Guidelines

Last updated: Jan 2026

Your safety is important to us. The following Safety Guidelines are provided to help users of AFIT Digital Market (ADM) have safer and more secure interactions when buying, selling, or offering services on the Platform. By using ADM, you agree to follow these safety recommendations.

1. General Safety Advice

  • AFIT Digital Market is an advertising and listing platform only. We do not physically inspect goods or services.
  • Always use your best judgment when interacting with vendors or buyers.
  • If an offer looks too good to be true, it probably is.

2. Meeting Vendors or Buyers

  • Whenever possible, meet in public, well-lit, and busy areas within the campus.
  • Avoid meeting in isolated or unfamiliar locations.
  • Inform a friend or roommate before meeting someone for a transaction.
  • Do not go alone if you feel unsureâ€”take someone with you.

3. Payments & Transactions

  • Do not make full payments before seeing the product or receiving the service.
  • Avoid sending advance payments to unknown vendors.
  • ADM does not guarantee refunds for transactions made outside the Platform.
  • Keep records of chats, receipts, and transaction details.

4. Online Communication Safety

  • Be cautious when sharing personal information such as your home address, ID cards, or bank details.
  • Communicate clearly and keep conversations professional.
  • ADM interactions may redirect to third-party platforms such as WhatsApp; exercise caution when continuing discussions there.

5. Vendor Verification

  • Some vendors may display a verification badge, which indicates submitted information has been reviewed.
  • Verification does not guarantee product quality, honesty, or delivery.
  • Always carry out your own checks before making any commitment.

6. Buying Safely

  • Inspect items carefully before completing payment.
  • Ask questions about product condition, warranty, or return options.
  • Avoid rushed decisions or pressure tactics from sellers.

7. Selling Safely

  • Meet buyers in safe locations.
  • Do not hand over goods until payment is confirmed.
  • Be cautious of fake transfer alerts or screenshots.
  • Avoid sharing sensitive personal or financial details.

8. Prohibited & Risky Activities

Do not engage in:

  • Illegal transactions
  • Fraudulent activities
  • Requests involving stolen items
  • Activities that violate campus rules or local laws

Such activities may be reported and listings removed.

9. Reporting Issues

If you suspect fraud, harassment, or unsafe behavior:

  • Stop communication immediately
  • Do not proceed with the transaction
  • Report the vendor or listing to ADM through our official contact channels

10. Disclaimer

AFIT Digital Market is not responsible for losses, injuries, disputes, or damages resulting from interactions between users. All transactions are conducted at your own risk.

11. Stay Alert

Your safety comes first. Always trust your instincts and prioritize your well-being over completing a deal.

By using AFIT Digital Market, you acknowledge that you have read and understood these Safety Guidelines.
