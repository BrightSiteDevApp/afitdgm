About ADM

Welcome to AFIT Digital Market (ADM)

AFIT Digital Market (ADM) is a campus-focused online marketplace created to connect students, small business owners, and service providers within the AFIT community. Our goal is to make it easy for people to discover, promote, and access goods and services around them ‚Äî all in one trusted digital space.

ADM is designed to be simple, fast, and student-friendly. Whether you are looking for food, gadgets, services, accommodation, or academic support, ADM helps you find what you need without stress.

What We Do

AFIT Digital Market serves as a listing and discovery platform. We allow vendors and service providers to showcase their offerings, while buyers can easily browse, search, and contact vendors directly to complete transactions.

Please note that ADM does not directly sell products or process payments. All transactions and communications happen between buyers and vendors.

Our Mission

  • Support student entrepreneurs and small businesses.
  • Make buying and selling within the campus easier and safer.
  • Create a reliable digital marketplace tailored to campus life.
  • Encourage innovation and digital solutions within the student community.

Why AFIT Digital Market?

  • Campus-focused listings
  • Easy contact with vendors via direct communication
  • Simple and user-friendly interface
  • Promotes local student businesses
  • Designed with safety and transparency in mind
